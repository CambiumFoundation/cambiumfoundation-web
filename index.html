Our mission

Pioneering positive change through microloans.

At the heart of global progress lies the power of individual potential. We unlock that potential in underserved communities by providing microloans that empower people to lift up themselves and those around them.

Our nonprofit mission is to support people creating an improved future for themselves, their families, and their communities, by funding microloans for individuals in need.

Why microloans?

Microloans are more than financial assistance, they are a beacon of hope.

  • 01
    Empower entrepreneurs. Capital for those shut out of traditional banking lets budding entrepreneurs start and grow their businesses.
  • 02
    Boost local economies. As these businesses grow they create jobs, stimulate local economies, and build community.
  • 03
    Promote self-sufficiency. Microloans are not handouts. They are tools that help people become self-reliant, with pride and accomplishment.

Our name

Why
"Cambium"?

The Cambium Foundation draws its name from a powerful symbol in nature: the cambium layer of a tree. In botany, the cambium is a thin layer of tissue between the wood and the bark, responsible for the secondary growth of stems and roots. It is the part of the tree that lets it grow wider and stronger over time, not just expansion, but a fundamental strengthening that lets the tree reach higher, withstand the elements, and support its ecosystem.

This metaphor aligns with our mission. Just as the cambium fosters growth in a tree, the foundation supports the growth and strengthening of entrepreneurs in underserved communities worldwide. By providing microloans, we invest in the potential of individuals to grow their own businesses and contribute to the economic health of their communities.

Each loan, like the cambium's contribution to a tree, may seem small in isolation, but it plays a crucial role in a larger cycle of growth and sustainability. The entrepreneurs we support are enabled to expand their ventures, create employment, and strengthen their community's resilience, much like a tree supports life within its ecosystem.

The name encapsulates this philosophy of nurturing and growth. It reflects our commitment not just to financial support, but to fostering a sustainable, inclusive environment where entrepreneurs can thrive, innovate, and in turn support others, embodying the enduring, life-sustaining qualities of the cambium across the 81 countries we serve.

How are the funds distributed?

We work through well-known microfund providers like Kiva to handle the distribution of funds.

What portion of donations goes to the cause?

98% of all donations we receive are channeled directly to entrepreneurs. Thanks to our efficient team and low overhead, we commit more than many larger nonprofits.

Who do you support?

We focus on individuals with the greatest financial need across 81 countries, helping them start and grow businesses that strengthen their communities.
